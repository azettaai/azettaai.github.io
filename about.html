// THE PROBLEM

The Black Box Problem.

Every major AI model deployed today is a black box. Inputs go in, outputs come out, and no one — not even the engineers who built it — can explain why.

This creates compounding risk: models that hallucinate, discriminate, and fail silently in production. The only fix offered by the industry is more scale — which makes the problem worse, not better.

Physics Grounded AI takes a different path. By designing interpretability into the architecture from the start — not bolted on after the fact — every decision becomes traceable, every neuron becomes meaningful, and steering becomes direct.
